# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the Izel project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.
- Help keep discussions technical, collaborative, and welcoming.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or demeaning language.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional open-source setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, chats, and other official project communication channels.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Email izeno.contact@gmaill.com.
2. If needed, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.
3. Include links, screenshots, and relevant context when possible.

Maintainers will review reports promptly, keep handling as confidential as possible, and take appropriate corrective action.

## Enforcement Actions

Depending on severity, maintainers may:

- Request behavior changes.
- Remove or edit harmful content.
- Temporarily limit participation.
- Ban repeat or severe offenders.

## Good Faith

Maintainers will strive to evaluate reports fairly, act proportionally, and prioritize community safety.
